Frequently Asked Questions

How does thaeos save me up to 70% on my running costs?

We schedule your Fabric instances, by working directly with your business, and re-organise your workspaces to ensure you can still access all of your reports and models whilst Fabric is turned 'off'.

Can I access thaeos across multiple tenants, my business has multiple subsidiaries?

Yes, absolutely. Just pay for thaeos once, and you can manage all of your linked tenants.

How does the governance Platform page help me govern my tenant?

We've spent over 10 years working around Power BI, Microsoft Fabric and other data platform tooling. In doing this, we've created specific metrics and optics that ensure you can pro-actively manage your data platform - action leaks before someone tells you they're using a data source, never lose a report, whether someone deletes them in anger, be the one that notifies the business of any data mishaps - not the other way around.

Can anyone use thaeos?

As soon as you pay for thaeos, anyone with Power BI or Microsoft Fabric admin access in your tenant will be able to log in and use thaeos. Please note here, that if you restrict their access in your tenant, you'll also be restricting their access in thaeos.

Is the app available on all devices?

We're working on it, currently you can access thaeos on the web or within Microsoft Teams from your PC, laptop, iPad or similar sized devices, soon you'll be able to use thaeos on the go from your mobile.
